# Tier 2 Plan for Jordan

Jordan is a 5th grader who struggles with executive functioning problems and likely has some anxiety that interferes with work completion. He often refuses to work and shuts down during independent tasks. He may have an undiagnosed learning disability related to attention or processing issues.

## Strengths
- Good at group work.
- Likes science.

## Problem Summary
Jordan does not complete work and is frequently off task. This has been a chronic issue across subjects.

## Hypothesized Causes
Jordan likely has underlying ADHD-like symptoms and possibly avoidance behavior driven by anxiety. He also seems unmotivated and needs to be pushed.

## Interventions
- Provide extra time.
- Remind him frequently to stay focused.
- Have him come in during recess to finish anything not completed.
- Use behavior charts to track his behavior.

## Progress Monitoring
- Teacher observation.
- Grade reports.

## Timeline
Check in after a few weeks.
